Our Approach

Investment Strategy

We employ an opportunistic long/short equity and fixed income strategy to provide non-correlated absolute returns with a low risk profile. The process does not employ leverage, futures, options or any other “exotic” financial instruments, and our investment universe is comprised solely of U.S. listed securities. Our goal is to find the most timely, low risk, deep value long/short opportunities from a combination of internal or external screens, portfolio manager contacts, Wall Street research, original research and technical analysis.

Clients arrange for their own custody and account administration allowing the advisor (CDF) to solely provide investment management for a fee. This arrangement has the advantage of allowing clients real time access to portfolio holdings, performance and in person or electronic discussions with the advisor (CDF) at any time. There are no “lock-ups” and investment agreements are subject to the client’s needs and can be terminated by the client with 24 hour notice or less.

Leadership

Richard is the founding member of the Capital Defense Funds LLC, and has been in the investment business for over 25 years. Richard has managed absolute return portfolios since late 1990 with an extensive and proven track record of capital preservation. Richard started his career in 1980 at the Bank of Boston as an equity analyst. He rapidly rose to the role of portfolio manager while at the same time playing a key role in the spin-off of the bank’s institutional division in 1984 and eventual sale to United Asset Management in 1989. During this period Richard was the sole manager of a high profile small cap fund as well as numerous large value oriented institutional accounts totaling over $1 billion.

At United Asset Management in late 1990, Richard started a new product called the Capital Defense Portfolios focusing on producing positive returns in all market environments. This product grew to over $300 million before Richard left United Asset Management in 1996 to join Grantham, Mayo, Van Otterloo & Co. LLC, one of the most highly regarded money managers in the investment business. Richard continued working on both large cap value portfolios as well as a new product he started called The Absolute Return Fund, which was operated in the same manner as his previous capital preservation oriented product. Richard left GMO in May of 2005 to start his own investment company. Richard is a CFA charter holder and received his MBA from the St. University of New York.
